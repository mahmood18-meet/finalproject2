I have such a messy life that couse me a lot of problems at school, home and MEET. You are probably asking yourself right now, what is MEET? well, MEET is a program who gather the leaders of the future who came from two different backgrounds, communities that have a lot going on between them, have you ever heard about the israeli-palestinian conflict?
MEET is trying to reconnect these two sides by teaching us tools that help us like:
1) See the full picture, and understand the other perspective.
2) Recognize and treat a problem we face in less time.
3) Understand others, by starting with ourselves.
4) Push ourselves out of our confort zone to evolve, in order to meet our truly self, and be comfortable about it.
5) Reach to more people, and convince them with our ideas.
6) Be brave enough to be the first who stand and make a rael change.
and a lot more, all these things that i have mentioned above, we learnd them in the last two years out of three years in three different classes: entrepreneurship, computer scince and DU (Deeper Understanding). you can go and raed a lot more about MEET program in their official website.
